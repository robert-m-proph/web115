CourtSide's FizzBuzz!

This page displays a form for you to enter two words. Once you enter two words in the form and click the submit button, you will see the numbers 1-140. For every number divisible by 3, your first word will be displayed. For every number divisible by 5, your second word will display. If the number is divisible by both 5 and 3, both words will be displayed. Lastly, if the number isn't divisible by 3, 5, or both, just the number will be displayed.
